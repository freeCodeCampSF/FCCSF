Welcome to freeCodeCamp San Francisco!

We're a group of coders in various places in our learning journey and we support each other through online meetups several times per week which you can find on Meetup. Most of our group members are currently learning full stack development and are working with JavaScript and React. You can always join in on the conversation in our active Slack group and enjoy our supportive community any time. Join our community of over 4.5k on Facebook and follow us on Instagram. You can also see what the group is working on over on Github. All of our meetups are currently online so we welcome coders from anywhere to join us. We hope to see you at an in-person meetup in the near future!

Contribute

This is an open source project intended as a learning opportunity for members of the freeCodeCamp San Francisco group so there might be mistakes. If you find a problem and are technically inclined you can open an issue over on github, or message us on any of the listed platforms to let us know. We appreciate your input!
