About to Free Code Camp San Francisco!

We're a group of coders in various places in our learning journey and we support each other through online meetups several times per week which you can find on Meetup. Most of our group members are currently learning full stack development and are working with JavaScript and React.

ou can always join in on the conversation in our active Slack group and enjoy our supportive community any time. Join our community of over 4.5k on Facebook and follow us on Instagram. You can also see what the group is working on over on and follow us on Github. All of our meetups are currently online so we welcome coders from anywhere to join us. We hope to see you at an in-person meetup in the near future!

Can Anyone Join?

Yes! We welcome anyone from anywhere who is interested in coding. As long as they stay within the Code of Conduct.

By joining us you are agreeing to follow the freeCodeCamp Code of Conduct
